AI Learning Journey

Congratulations! Learning about AI can be daunting but you are brave and want to learn more! We have designed 10 unique challenges to acquaint you with AI.

They are low stakes. Low risk. We don't track your progress - you can go at your own pace.

Each week, you'll receive an email with an activity designed to introduce you to different ways to use AI. We'll start with a brief foundational tour of AI, move into some easy-to-do (and fun!) activities to help you gain exposure to AI, and then move on to more practical ideas!

And hopefully you'll have fun along the way.

We have identified four stages of AI use:

Recognize:

Identify where AI exists in your world and how it can be useful

Investigate:

Explore AI tools and their potential

Supercharge:

Leverage AI to enhance creativity, productivity, and decision-making potentiating your actions

Exchange:

Collaborate with AI in a dynamic, interactive way, blending human expertise with AI's capabilities
