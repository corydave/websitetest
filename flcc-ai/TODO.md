### You Can't AI Wrong | AI like No One is Watching
From Aaron: Prompt engineering is essentially an iterative process of optimization to minimize error to a point that you find an acceptable level of error. There is no “right way” to do anything. The model doesn’t care how you approach it. There’s no shame in repeating your core instructions over and over so that the model makes fewer errors. That’s just optimization towards local minima.
