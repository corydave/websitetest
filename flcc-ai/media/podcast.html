About the Show

The Immersive Lens

AI Conversations Worth Having

The Immersive Lens goes beyond the headlines to explore what AI is actually doing in classrooms, businesses, and communities. Each episode features candid conversations with practitioners, educators, and innovators who are working with these tools every day — not theorists, but people in the trenches.

Whether you're just getting started with AI or you're knee-deep in implementation, every episode is designed to give you something you can actually use. We dig into the wins, the failures, and the surprising moments in between.
