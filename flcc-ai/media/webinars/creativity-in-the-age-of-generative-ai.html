About This Webinar

Since its rapid rise in 2022, generative AI has been promoted as a tool for increasing efficiency, quality, and automation, becoming a prolific producer of content. But what does creativity mean in this new landscape, and what distinctly human qualities shape the earliest stages of an AI-assisted process? Join us for an exploration of how human imagination, lived experience, and ethical judgment intersect with generative AI, and what it means to be creative and authentic alongside these emerging technologies.
