About This Webinar

NotebookLM is a Google product that acts as a source of truth for your projects. Upload documents, URLs, YouTube videos, and more to create a personalized knowledge base. When you “chat” with the AI inside your notebook, all responses are taken directly from your sources — no hallucinations, no guessing.

In this session we’ll walk through real examples of NotebookLM in action: summarizing long documents, answering questions about uploaded content, generating study guides, and even creating audio overviews (podcast-style summaries) of your materials.

What You’ll Learn

  • How to set up a NotebookLM notebook and add sources
  • Practical prompting strategies for getting the most out of your sources
  • How to generate summaries, FAQs, and outlines automatically
  • Creating audio overviews from your documents
  • Use cases for educators, researchers, and organizations
